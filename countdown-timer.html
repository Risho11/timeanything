About this timer

Set the duration in hours, minutes, and seconds, or use one of the quick preset buttons. Click Start and the timer counts down to zero, then plays an audio alert. You can pause and resume without losing progress.

How to use it

  1. Click a preset button, or enter a custom duration using the hour/minute/second fields.
  2. Click Start to begin the countdown.
  3. Click Pause to hold the timer. Click Start to resume.
  4. Click Reset to go back to the set duration.

FAQ

Can I set the countdown in hours, minutes, and seconds?
Yes — enter any combination. For example, 1 hour 30 minutes 0 seconds for a 90-minute timer.
Does it beep when time runs out?
Yes — an audio alert plays when the countdown hits zero. Keep your volume on.
Are there quick presets?
Yes — common durations like 5, 10, 15, 20, and 30 minutes have one-click preset buttons.
Can I use this as a meeting timer?
Yes. Set it to your meeting length and it will alert you when time is up.
