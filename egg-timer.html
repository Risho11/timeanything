Egg cooking times

Timings assume eggs are placed into boiling water from room temperature. Results will vary slightly by egg size and stove.

  • 3 minutes: Soft-boiled, runny yolk
  • 4 minutes: Soft-boiled, semi-liquid yolk
  • 6 minutes: Medium — yolk just set, still slightly soft in center
  • 8 minutes: Medium-hard, yolk mostly firm
  • 10 minutes: Hard-boiled, fully cooked yolk

How to use it

  1. Click a preset or enter a custom duration.
  2. Start the timer when you put food in the pot or oven.
  3. The timer beeps when time is up.

FAQ

How long for a soft-boiled egg?
About 6 minutes from boiling water. Use the Soft-boiled preset for a quick start.
Can I set a custom time?
Yes — enter any minutes and seconds for custom kitchen timing, not just eggs.
Does it alert when done?
Yes — an audio beep plays when the timer hits zero.
Can I use this for other cooking?
Absolutely. It works for pasta, rice, steaming vegetables, or any timed kitchen task.
