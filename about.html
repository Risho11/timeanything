About TimeAnything

TimeAnything is a free collection of online timer tools. Everything runs in your browser — no account, no download, no data sent anywhere.

What's here

The tools cover the most common timer needs: Pomodoro sessions for productivity, a stopwatch with lap timing, countdown timers with audio alerts, HIIT interval timers, a live clock, an egg/kitchen timer with cooking presets, a countdown-to-date display, and a simple deep work focus timer.

How the timers work

All timers use Date.now() to measure elapsed real-world time rather than counting raw intervals. This means they remain accurate even when the browser tab is in the background or the device is under load. Audio alerts use the Web Audio API — no audio files needed.
