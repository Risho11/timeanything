About this clock

This clock displays your local system time as reported by your browser. It updates every second and shows the current time, date, day of the week, and your local timezone.

Toggle between 12-hour (AM/PM) and 24-hour format using the checkbox below the clock.

Common uses

  • Quickly checking the current time without unlocking your phone
  • Keeping a visible clock open in a second monitor or browser tab
  • Verifying what timezone your browser is set to
  • Display clocks for presentations or video recordings

FAQ

Is this my local time?
Yes. The clock reads time directly from your browser and device, so it reflects your local timezone.
Can I display a different timezone?
Not currently — the clock shows your device's system time. For world clock functionality, your browser's developer tools or a dedicated world clock site would be needed.
How accurate is it?
It is as accurate as your device's system clock. Most computers sync their clocks via NTP and are accurate to within a second.
